About

What is Sportance?

Sportance was founded in 2017 with the aim to help athletes, teams, coaches and support staff with Sport Psychology interventions and techniques. Understanding and using these techniques allow sports performers and support staff to be the best they can be. Often athletes and coaches only focus on the physical training to help them improve and therefore, neglect the mental side of training. Sportance’s aim is to educate, support and implement interventions to help sports professionals across the board. We want sports professionals to be the best they can be by unlocking the power of their brain.

What is Sport Psychology?

Sport Psychology uses Psychological knowledge and skills to address optimal sports performance. It uses a variety of skills and interventions such as imagery, SMART goals and positive self-talk to help sports professionals achieve optimal sports performance. Sport Psychology has many advantages such as:

  • Increased motivation
  • Helping the athlete/team/coach to be more target/goal orientated
  • Improved attention
  • Understanding how to perform under pressure
  • Develop effective communication skills

Services

How does it work?

Sportance offers a variety of services that are tailored to meet each individual’s, teams or coach’s needs. After contact is made a free 60 minute intial consultation will take place. This will be face to face and be a ‘get to know you’ session. This is the start of a close working relationship which allows the clients to feel comfortable.

The meeting will consist of an initial assessment and there will be an opportunity to ask as many questions as you like which is important in determining whether Sport Psychology and Sportance is right for you. After the initial meeting our experts will set up an intervention plan tailored to your needs. This will allow you to see the journey right from the start. From time to time, depending on progress this plan may change to stay in line with the progress being made.

What services are offered?

1-1 consultations - 60 minute session focuses on helping you improve. Whether that be dealing with pressure, setting targets and working towards them or help when returning from injury. Each session is planned with you in mind and tailored to suit all of your needs and help you achieve optimal performance.

Group workshops - typically a workshop will last a day and is suitable for small sports teams of 15 people who are looking to work together to improve. Throughout the day, there will be a range or activity to help the team work together to achieve the same goals. This can then be applied to training and competition situations.*larger teams can be split across a number of days

Educational workshops - this is designed to educate athletes and coaches about the way Sport psychology works and it’s benefits. These can last from 1 hour to 5 hours.

How can it benefit you?

Sport Psychology has a vast range of benefits that can help you achieve optimal performance. These include:

  • Increased motivation
  • Clear targets/goals to work towards
  • Improved for focus and attention
  • Improved well-being
  • Confidence when returning from injury
  • Manage stress and anxiety
  • Improved team work and cohesion
