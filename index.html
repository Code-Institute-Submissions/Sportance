What is Sportance

Sportance was founded in 2017 with the aim to help athletes, teams, coaches and support staff with Sport Psychology interventions and techniques. Understanding and using these techniques allow sports performers and support staff to be the best they can be. Often athletes and coaches only focus on the physical training to help them improve and therefore, neglect the mental side of training. We want sports professionals to be the best they can be by unlocking the power of their brain.

What is Sport Psychology

Sport Psychology uses Psychological knowledge and skills to address optimal sports performance. It uses a variety of skills and interventions such as imagery, SMART goals and positive self-talk to help sports professionals achieve optimal sports performance. Sport Psychology has many advantages such as:

  • Increased motivation
  • Helping the athlete/team/coach to be more target/goal orientated
  • Improved attention
  • Understanding how to perform under pressure
  • Develop effective communication skills
